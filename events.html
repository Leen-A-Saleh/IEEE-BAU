spectrum lab 4

the spectrum lab 4 conference, organized by our student branch, proved to be an exceptional event. it was a large-scale conference catering to a broad audience of students specializing in engineering and those interested in technology across jordanian universities. the conference featured a diverse lineup of speakers from various domains, including artificial intelligence, robotics, renewable energy, and biotechnology, who shared their insights, experiences, and advice with the attendees. in addition, the conference included numerous events and competitions. it was highly successful, receiving positive feedback from both participants and sponsors, and garnered media coverage from multiple outlets.

the faculty of huson

faculty of huson hosted a special event to showcase the achievements of its societies and their members. the event featured presentations, demonstrations, and exhibitions by various societies . the event also invited speakers from different fields and backgrounds to share their insights and experiences with the attendees. the event was a great opportunity for students, faculty, staff, and guests to learn more about the societies, their projects, and how they can get involved.
